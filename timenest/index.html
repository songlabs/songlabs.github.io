TimeNest

TimeNest is a local-first iOS calendar app for month, week, and day scheduling, shift records, work-time notes, and public-holiday subscriptions.

About TimeNest

  • Month, week, and day calendar views
  • Timed and all-day events
  • Shift templates and work-time records
  • Public-holiday subscriptions
  • Japanese, Simplified Chinese, English, and Korean interface resources
  • Local-first data storage on the device

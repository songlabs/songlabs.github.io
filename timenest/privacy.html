TimeNest Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TimeNest handles information when you use the app. TimeNest is designed as a local-first calendar app. Calendar data created by the user is stored on the device by default.

Information You Create in the App

TimeNest allows you to create and manage calendar events, all-day events, shift records, work-time records, settings, and public-holiday subscription settings.

This user-created calendar data is stored locally on your device. TimeNest does not currently provide account sign-in, cloud synchronization, or a developer-operated server for uploading your calendar data.

Local Storage

TimeNest stores app data such as events, shift records, work records, display settings, language settings, holiday subscription choices, and cached holiday data in the app's local container on your device.

If you uninstall the app, iOS normally removes the app's local container and the data stored inside it.

Widgets and App Group Data

If TimeNest widgets are enabled, the app may share a limited local snapshot of calendar information with its widget extension through Apple's App Group mechanism. This is used to display calendar information on your device and is not a cloud synchronization feature.

Public-Holiday Subscriptions

TimeNest can download public-holiday data from the holiday source selected in the app's settings. When you test, refresh, or enable a holiday subscription, the app may send HTTPS requests to the selected public ICS provider in order to download holiday calendar data.

The downloaded holiday data is cached locally on your device for calendar display.

Advertising

TimeNest may use Google Mobile Ads to display banner advertisements. When advertising is enabled, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may process information such as device information, advertising-related identifiers, ad interactions, diagnostics, performance data, and other information as described by Google and required for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and related advertising operations.

The exact App Store privacy disclosures and tracking configuration depend on the final production advertising setup. TimeNest does not sell user-created calendar data.

Third-Party Services

TimeNest may interact with the following third-party services depending on the features you use:

  • Public ICS holiday providers selected in the app's holiday subscription settings
  • Google Mobile Ads, if advertising is enabled

Children's Privacy

TimeNest is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not currently provide account registration or developer-operated cloud storage.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when TimeNest changes its features, data handling, advertising configuration, or legal requirements. The latest version will be posted on this page.

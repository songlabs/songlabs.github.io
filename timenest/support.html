TimeNest Support

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Thank you for using TimeNest. This page provides support information for the TimeNest iOS app.

Contact

For support, feedback, or bug reports, please contact: songlabs.dev@gmail.com

When reporting a problem, please include your device model, iOS version, app version, the language setting you are using, and a short description of the issue.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my calendar data stored?

TimeNest is designed as a local-first app. User-created calendar events, shift records, work records, and settings are stored on your device by default.

Does TimeNest require an account?

No. The current version of TimeNest does not require account registration or sign-in.

Does TimeNest provide cloud sync?

No. The current version does not provide developer-operated cloud synchronization.

How do public-holiday subscriptions work?

TimeNest can download public-holiday data from the holiday source selected in Settings. Downloaded holiday data is cached locally on your device.

Why is my widget not updating immediately?

iOS controls when widgets refresh. If the widget does not update immediately, try opening TimeNest, checking that the relevant data is visible in the app, and waiting for iOS to refresh the widget.

How can I change the app language?

TimeNest supports Japanese, Simplified Chinese, English, Korean, and a system-language mode. You can change the language from the app's settings.

How can I report a bug?

Please email the support address above with a description of the issue, reproduction steps if possible, and screenshots if they help explain the problem.
